    Free Fire sensitivity settings (recommended) Your preferred Free Fire or Free Fire Max sensitivity setting will depend a lot on your playstyle. If you play in the mid to long range with an auto rifle, then the best sensitivity settings for this playstyle will be different from that of long-range sniper rifles. We have listed recommended sensitivity settings in Free Fire for various play styles.

    Free Fire sensitivity settings for close range
  • General – 95
  • Red Dot – 90
  • 2X Scope – 85
  • 4X Scope – 75
  • Sniper Scope – 58
  • Free Look – 67

    • Free Fire sensitivity settings for mid range
  • General – 100
  • Red Dot – 80
  • 2X Scope – 70

  • Free Look – 58
